## Socrates Discipline

Socrates is installed in this project. The full protocol lives in `RALPH.md`
at the project root — read it at the start of every session before doing
anything else.

Before commits on non-trivial changes: spawn the `code-critic` agent
(foreground, opus model) and address findings in at most 2 rounds.

All other discipline (role triage, phase sequence, handoff/ADR rules,
`.msgs/` inbox) lives in RALPH.md. Do not duplicate it here.
